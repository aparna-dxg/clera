FAQ's

Frequently Asked Questions About
Clera Windows + Doors

We understand that project managers and property owners have specific preferences for their windows and doors; this can be regarding the look, the level of privacy, and the level of security offered by the window or door.

One of the main benefits of securing a supply of windows and doors from Clera is the thorough level of customization we offer. You can customize patio doors, entrance doors, and all types of windows.

Patio Door Customization

When it comes to patio doors, you will be able to customize orders under the following parameters:

  • Number of panels - Choose from various door panel number options such as 2 panels (one sliding), 3 panels (one sliding),
    3 panels (two sliding), 4 panels (with two sliding), and more.
  • Grids on the patio door’s glass - This can be gridless, 3x6, 4x6, contour 3x4, contour 3x5, and so on.
  • Handles - Choose from handle styles like standard handles, Euro handles, and the 9900 handles.
  • Colours - Exquisite door colours await such as clay, blue-white, taupe, forest green, Venetian red, and more!

See these customization options for yourself on ourpatio door interactive Virtual Designer!

Front Entry Door Customization

As for entrance doors, you will be able to customize your order under these parameters:

  • Entrance door configuration- Determine your front door configuration - whether it’s single door, single door with one sidelight (left or right), single door with two sidelights, or double door with/without sidelights.
  • Door glass - Choose if you’d like the door to be with glass or without glass. And if you choose a door with glass, customize further by choosing from options like stained glass, V-groove glass, Silkscreen glass, and Textured glass (Fluid or Edge).
  • Door styles - Choose from our elegant door style collections like Shaker, Design, Prestige, and Classic.
  • Colours - Select from chic front door colours like Cashmere, Wedgewood blue, Sierra, Country Red, and more!

See these customization options for yourself on our door interactive Virtual Designer!

Window Customization

Go beyond customizing window sizes and window types (e.g. casement windows, awning windows, bay windows, bow windows, hung tilt windows); finetune your window orders using these additional customization parameters:

  • Grille options - Choose if you’d like grille-less windows or one with grilles; you can further customize by selecting from various window grille choices like Brass Prairie Grille, Colonial Grillem Flat Pewter Grille, and so on
  • Glass options - Select the type of glass you want your window order to have - whether it’s clear, frosted, tinted, mirrored glass, and so on.

View our different window styles on our online catalogue.
